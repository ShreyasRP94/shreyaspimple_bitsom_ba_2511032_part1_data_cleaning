# shreyaspimple_bitsom_ba_2511032_part1_data_cleaning

## Problem Summary - 

Dataset from a retail company is provided.

The company has exported order-level sales data from multiple internal systems. The raw dataset contains issues such as inconsistent text formatting, date format problems, duplicate records, missing values, invalid discounts, calculation mismatches, and order status inconsistencies.

In this project a clean, validated, analysis-ready version of the data along with summary reports have been prepared, that can be used for business review.

## Key business insights

1. Data Standardization Enables Better Business Analytics - After cleaning, the dataset became analysis-ready, allowing stakeholders to generate consistent reports, identify trends, and make informed business decisions with greater confidence.
2. Data Quality Issues Significantly Impact Reporting Accuracy - The raw dataset contained inconsistencies in dates, order statuses, and text formatting, which could lead to inaccurate business reports and unreliable decision-making. Standardizing and cleaning the data improved overall data integrity and reporting reliability.
3. Sales and Profit Calculation Errors Can Misstate Financial Performance - Validating business rules and recalculating financial metrics ensures accurate revenue and profitability analysis.
